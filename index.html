Gustavo Penha

I am a research scientist at Spotify, with a PhD from TU Delft. My work spans information retrieval, recommender systems, natural language processing, and machine learning. I am passionate about building systems that connect people with content (and creators) by understanding their information needs and preferences. I am interested in the entire information-seeking process and in designing and evaluating systems that support it.
